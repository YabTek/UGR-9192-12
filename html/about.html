My background

My name is Yeabsira Tekuamwork.I was born in 1994 at a little town,Woreilu.It is located in the south wollo zone of Amhara region.My childhood was very interesting. It was full of fun and play.I have completed my elementary and my high school upto grade 10 there.My childhood was very interesting.But after grade 11,I came to Addis Ababa and took a university entrance examination here at Addis Ababa.When I came here,everything was new for me.Adapting the environment,meeting new friends was difficult.My mother helped me alot in my education.She wants me to become successful and she tried many ways to make me a cleaver student since my early ages.

Now?

Now,I am a sofware engineering student at Addis Ababa university.I love the course so much and I love learning here because it is a well known university in Ethiopia.As the teachers are highly qualified,they give us a new insight of the course everytime.I am sure this will help me become successful and qualified.

My strength

One of my strengths is honesty.My mother always tells me to be honest and I think I have developed this discipline.I choose to be honest in my every activities.I am also good at time management.Managing time is a key for living life in organized way.That is why I always try to use my time properly.Self criticism is my other strength.I want to be responsible for my own actions.Nobody will take care of my mistakes,in many ways I don't blame others for my wrong actions.Rather I will try to solve it by correcting my flaws. And also am kind of strong person for what I belived to do.I want to accomplish my plans without others distruction.

My weakness

I do have weaknesses that I have to overcome and improve.One of my weaknesses is lack of confidence.My lack of confidence has made me underestimate my self in many situations.I am also not good at communication.Most of the time I afraid to talk to people specially for the first time.But in this field communication is very essential, I have to improve this.Lack of patience is my other weakness.I want things to become like I thought them to be with in a short period of time.
