My background

My name is Yeabsira Tekuamwork.I was born in 1994 at a little town,Woreilu.It is located in the south wollo zone of Amhara region.My childhood was very interesting. It was full of fun and play.I have completed my elementary and my high school there. And then I took my university entrance examination and got a good score.So, I was able to join the number one university in our country that is Addis Ababa University.

Now?

Now,I am a sofware engineering student at Addis Ababa university.I love my field so much and I love learning here because it is a well known university in Ethiopia.As the teachers are highly qualified,they give us a new insight of the course everytime.I am sure this will help me become successful and qualified.
